How It Started

Built with Purpose & Passion

Mapengo Innovations is an online-based technology company founded by Novice Nhlovo Mathebula, an ALX-trained Software Engineer. We build digital solutions that empower businesses through modern websites, custom software, and mobile applications — delivered entirely online.

Novice started Mapengo Innovations to bridge the gap between businesses and accessible, high-quality digital solutions. Our online model means we can serve clients across South Africa and beyond, without boundaries.

From idea to deployment, we build with purpose, passion, and precision. Our structured 5-step process ensures every project is professional, efficient, and exceeds expectations.
