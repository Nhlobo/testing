Our Origin

How We Started

Mapengo Innovations was founded by Novice Nhlovo Mathebula, an ALX Software Engineering graduate with a passion for building technology that solves real problems for South African businesses.

After seeing how many local businesses were being left behind in the digital economy — either because good development was too expensive or the solutions on offer didn’t fit the local market — Novice set out to change that.

Today Mapengo Innovations delivers professional-grade websites, mobile apps, and custom digital systems at prices that make sense for South African SMEs.
