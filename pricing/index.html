Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Are these prices fixed or can they change?

The prices shown are starting prices ("from"). The final quote depends on your specific requirements, number of features, integrations needed, and design complexity. We always provide a detailed, fixed quote before starting work so there are no surprises.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work and the remaining 50% on delivery. For larger projects, a staged payment schedule is available. All payment terms are clearly outlined in your contract.

Do your prices include hosting?

Hosting is not included in the listed prices but can be arranged as an add-on. We can advise on the best hosting solution for your project and budget, from shared hosting to dedicated cloud infrastructure.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. We build with growth in mind. You can always start with a Basic plan and upgrade to add more features, pages, or functionality as your business grows.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept EFT bank transfers and selected mobile payment methods. All payments are in South African Rand (ZAR). Receipts and invoices are issued for every transaction.

Do you offer maintenance after delivery?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your site or app updated, secure, and running smoothly. Ask about our monthly maintenance plans when requesting a quote.
